The History of Thomas Crapper

  • Thomas Crapper was born in 1836.
  • Thomas grew up plumbing.
  • In 1853 he became a Journeyman plumber.

How the magic happened

In the 1861, Crapper was a sanitary engineer, with his own brass foundry and various other shops in England. There Thomas began to build many different plumbing supplys with his 9 patents. 3 of the 9 were water closet improvements that helped shape what we know as the toilet today.

"

The 1880's for Crapper

In the 1880's Prince Edward purchased a home and requested the services of Thomas Crapper & Co. to supply the plumbing for his home, including 30 lavatories thus giving Thomas his first Royal notice. This job only furthered his name in infamy in relation to the toilet.
